UBC Literature Etc. is a non-profit UBC student organization that focuses on literature, music, photography, film, and fine arts. We are the gathering place for lovers of all form of art. One of our most notable endeavours is the publication of the chinese magazine, leMook. While leMook has an emphasis in literature we have historically showcased other forms of arts including photography, illustrations, and much more. In our pursuit of art appreciation, we organize a variety of events each year. including MiniStudio, LeRococo, and the Vancouver Taiwanese Film Festival.
