Sometimes all a photographer really needs is another photographer buddy to speak photography with. MiniStudio is an annual gathering of photographers, professionals and enthusiasts alike, hosted by UBCLE. Each year we design numerous sets and work together with professional makeup artists and models of experience level to bring photographers a truly unique experience.

Not only will you get to meet fellow photography lovers, we also invite experienced photographers to come and share their tips on photography. Do not miss out on this awesome event where your creativity is your only limit.
